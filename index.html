Welcome to SDH Project Services

SDH, combined with our strategic alliance of suppliers and sub contractors provide our customers with world class services covering all stages of the development cycle, backed by our integrated Quality, Environment and Health & Safety systems and our robust approach to identifying and managing risk.

From support on the business case preparation and advice on key issues to design, implementation, construction, operations, maintenance and managed services provision, we provide every service and skill required to deliver innovative, inclusive and sustainable solutions for our clients.

In today’s market, differentiation is a must and this enables our business to have diverse revenue streams in a highly competitive market place. The SDH brand has grown consistently since it was formed in 2001 and offers clients in both the public and private sectors a solution focussed approach for their telecommunications requirements such as Fixed and Mobile Network Design, Construction, Network Services, Security & Surveillance Systems, Preventative Maintenance Support, Managed Services, Project Management and the expertise to deliver International Turnkey Telecommunications Projects.
